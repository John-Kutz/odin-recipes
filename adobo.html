How to make adobo

Description

This is another Filipino meal that I like to make on the weekend. It is the national dish of the Philippines which has multiple variations like pork, beef, or chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 3 pieces of dried bay leaves
  • 8 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons of white vinegar
  • 5 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 3 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of whole peppercorn
