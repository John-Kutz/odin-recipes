How to make sinigang

Description

Sinigang is one of my favorite Filipino dishes that I cook occasionally. It is a sour soup dish that can be made with different types of meat, which usually is pork, beef, or shrimp.For this one we will be using my favorite variant of this dish, which is a fish dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. milkfish, cleaned and sliced
  • 1 pack of Knorr Sinigang na Sampaloc Mix (Original)
  • 1 bunch fresh kangkong leaves
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 quarts water
