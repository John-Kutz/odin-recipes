How to make spaghetti

Description

This is one of my go to meals to cook for dinner as it is an easy dish to make. It is a pasta dish that I like tweak by adding meatballs and some chicken, however for this recipe we will be just doing a plain dish.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces dried spaghetti
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 jar of pasta sauce
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil
